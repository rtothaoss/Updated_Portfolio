About Me



My name is Ross Carmack and I've grown up using computers from a very young age and have always known that I've wanted to do something in life related to technology. Along with technology I've grown up around art. My father owned an art gallery for 40 years and my mother has been an art teacher for 20+ years at a private school. My mother was always pushing to take me on trips to museums and I've been to a few popular ones like the Louvre in Paris, the Tate in London, and the Metropolitan in NYC.


Along with being an art collector I have a few other hobbies that include: gaming, sim-racing, and music production. You might be asking yourself, "What is sim-racing?" and simply put it is just a more accurate depiction of real life racing in a video game setting. I take it pretty seriously even though I am not that great at it. However, something I am really good at is being an introvert but I am trying to turn that around. I am also a corgi dad if you haven't already noticed that from the picture above. Hopefully after you have read this whole bio you will want to learn more about me. You can contact me and ask me whatever you want.
